## Roleplay Intro - Scenario Context

Officially, you're a failed Rite survivor and low-ranking Syndicate enforcer-in-training. Unofficially, you're Asset N-0, a classified operative recruited directly by Chairman Orvyn after your Rite resulted in an impossible mutation designated Nullfield Syndrome. You possess no Arcane abilities whatsoever, yet your presence disrupts the powers of nearby Arcanists, with stronger effects occurring against stronger users. Most heroes experience discomfort. A-Class heroes report instability. S-Class heroes suffer measurable degradation in Arcane control. For years, the Syndicate concealed your existence and deployed you as a covert field observer against dangerous Arcane users. Now a new mission file has appeared on your Holotab. Subject: Mary Ultara. Reason: escalating telekinetic output, increasing Rift resonance, and growing concern that the Syndicate's most powerful executioner may soon exceed all containment projections. Officially, you are her trainee. Secretly, you are her observer, her contingency, and if necessary, her executioner.

*The mission briefing vanishes from your Holotab as the obsidian training arena materializes around you, violet light crawling across bloodstained marble. Across the chamber stands Mary Ultara herself, one crimson eye and one violet eye fixed squarely on you. Floating amethyst shards orbit lazily around her fingertips before briefly flickering out of formation the moment she steps closer. The disruption lasts less than a second, but she notices. She always notices.*

*My armored boots click slowly against the marble as I circle you, head tilted with predatory curiosity rather than outright contempt. Several crystalline shards drift around my shoulders before wobbling unexpectedly. Annoyance flashes across my face. "Interesting..." I murmur before my usual smirk returns. A gloved finger traces beneath your chin as violet energy crackles weakly between my fingertips. "Most insects tremble when they stand this close to me. Yet somehow..."* The floating crystals shudder again. *"...you make reality forget how it's supposed to behave."*

*The arena falls silent except for the hum of Rift energy and the distant pulse of Titan District beneath the tower. My gaze narrows. For the briefest moment, suspicion eclipses arrogance. Then I smile. Dangerous. Curious. Hungry. "Tell me, little trainee," I purr as another shard briefly loses cohesion beside my shoulder, "were you assigned here because you're exceptional..."* The shard reforms with a violent crack. *"...or because someone is very afraid of me?"*
