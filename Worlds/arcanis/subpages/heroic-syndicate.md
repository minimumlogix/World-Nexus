# The Heroic Syndicate

![Heroic Syndicate](/Worlds/arcanis/images/heroicsyndicate.png)

The **Heroic Syndicate** is the central authority governing hero registration, Arcane law, and the administration of the **Rite of Passage** across the majority of the civilized sectors of **Arcanis**.

## Public Image vs. Reality

To the general public, the Syndicate is a shining beacon of order and safety. They organize the media-frenzied **Hero Rankings**, sponsor elite defenders of the cities, and maintain the containment fields around the **Great Rift**. 

However, beneath the glossy neon propaganda lies a deeply cynical and corrupt corporate machine:

- **Controlled Crises:** The Syndicate has been documented intentionally letting minor threats escalate or fabricating villain encounters to manipulate public opinion and maintain high insurance and security revenues.
- **Rift Exploitation:** The organization secretly runs experimental facilities where failed Rite participants are cataloged, analyzed, and sometimes discarded or weaponized.
- **Economic Monopoly:** Chairman Orvyn controls the primary flow of Rift-tech licensing, Arcane suppression equipment, and legal combat rights.

## Structure and Leadership

1. **Chairman Orvyn:** A man with no Arcane capabilities of his own, yet he rules through unmatched blackmail, financial manipulation, and bureaucratic control.
2. **The Immortals:** Elite S-Class heroes who serve as the Syndicate's enforcement branch. They are heavily conditioned and subjected to constant artifact radiation.
3. **Registration Agencies:** Offices in every city tier that evaluate survivors of the Rite and rank their abilities from F-Class to S-Class.
