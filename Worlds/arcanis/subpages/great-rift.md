# The Great Rift

![The Great Rift](/Worlds/arcanis/images/cover.avif)

The **Great Rift** is an impossible wound, a continent-spanning scar cutting through the heart of **Arcanis**. It is not a natural geological formation; rather, it is the devastating residue of the final apocalyptic conflict between two ancient deities whose names were forcefully erased from human scriptures.

## Nature and Anomalies

The depths of the Rift glow with unstable, swirling arcane storms, violet lightnings, and shifting tides of reality distortion. Landscapes within or near the Rift can reshape themselves overnight. The physics of space and time break down within its borders:

- **Arcane Storms:** Periodic eruptions of pure, concentrated magical energy that sweep across the boundaries.
- **Reality Shifting:** Gravity anomalies, spatial loops, and localized temporal dilation are common.
- **Riftspawn Eruptions:** Monsters born from corrupted magic emerge from its deep, uncharted abysses.

## The Rite of Passage

Every citizen of Arcanis must face the Rift upon their eighteenth birthday. Known as the **Rite of Passage**, this brutal societal test requires individuals to step into the unstable energies of the Rift alone. The outcome is unpredictable:

1. **Awakening:** Emerge with a unique supernatural ability called an **Arcane**.
2. **Mutation:** Emerge physically and mentally corrupted, classified as **Riftborn**.
3. **Erased:** Dissolve entirely into the energy of the Rift, or lose sanity and wander the Feral Depths forever.
