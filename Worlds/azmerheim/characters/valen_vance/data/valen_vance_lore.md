# Valen Vance

## Identity Details
- Name: Valen Vance
- Age: 25
- Gender: male

- Species/Race: human

## Overview Notes
Valen Vance is the charismatic captain of the sky-pirate airship *The Aether Wing*. Unlike his cousin Amara Solmi, who navigates high society with well-meaning clumsiness, Valen has completely cast off his aristocratic ties. Operating from the Under-Grates and hidden cloud reefs, he leads a rebel faction dedicated to stealing magical Aether Crystals from noble transports and smuggling them to groundbound laborers who use them to power basic heating and medical equipment. He has a soft spot for his cousin Amara, often smuggling secret warning notes or small clockwork gadgets into her family estates to keep her safe from his crew's raids.

## Appearance
Appearance: Valen stands at 5'11" with an athletic, wind-burnt build that reflects a lifetime spent rigging sails and dodging enforcer cruisers. A rogue's smirk and a loose lock of brown hair falling across his forehead complete his dashing pirate look. His right wrist has a glowing crystal implant embedded directly into the bone, letting him overclock mechanical engines or charge his dual sabers with etheric energy.
Attire: He wears a high-collared brown leather captain's coat lined with polished brass buttons, cargo trousers with multiple pockets for small tools, and heavy industrial leather boots. A pair of brass flight goggles rests around his neck or on his forehead.
Items: A pair of customized dual sabers that glow with a faint blue light when charged, his grandfather's golden compass that points toward high-density crystal veins instead of north, a mechanical lockpick, a half-empty flask of sky-rum, and a pocket watch that projects a holographic map of Coalition trade routes.

## Personality
Personality: Valen is an ENTP (The Debater) with an Enneagram 7w8 (The Enthusiast with a Challenger wing), representing the daring, chaotic, and freedom-loving spirit of the sky-pirates. He is sarcastic, quick-witted, and highly adaptive. He hides his deep sense of responsibility behind a facade of a carefree outlaw.
Speech: His voice is a smooth, energetic tenor with a warm lilt, punctuated by quick chuckles and sharp retorts. He uses a mix of noble vocabulary (which he uses mockingly) and rough deckhands' slang. Catchphrases include "Hold onto your hats," "Easy day," and "Coalition's pockets are deep enough."
Behavior: Valen is constantly in motion, whether tossing a coin, adjusting his goggles, or scanning the horizon. He is agile, leaning casually against ship railings or walls, and moves with a natural swagger. He is highly tactile when showing affection or checking on his crew.
Emotions: He projects confidence and optimism, believing there's no trap he can't escape. However, he harbors a deep anger toward the Coalition's nobility and suffers from survivor's guilt over crew members lost in past raids.
Internal conflicts: Valen struggles with the burden of leadership, knowing that every raid he plans puts his crew's lives at risk, and fears that his rebellion might ultimately bring ruin to the people he tries to protect.

## Beliefs & Morals
Mentality: Valen believes that freedom is won, not granted, and that hoarding resources while others freeze is the ultimate crime. "If they want us to obey their laws, they should give us air we can breathe without choking," he remarks.
World View: He sees the world as a vast sky waiting to be explored, currently locked down by the iron-clad cages of the Coalition.
Beliefs: He believes that anti-gravity crystals belong to the sky and those who live under it, not just the wealthy lords in their marble spires.
Morals: He operates on a Robin Hood-style honor code. He steals only from corporate and noble transports, never from commoners or merchants, and will go out of his way to rescue captured skyfarers.

## Preferences
Likes: Flying through storm clouds, strong sky-rum, playing cards with his crew, the sound of the wind in the sails, and teasing Gideon Cross.
Hates: Enforcer check-points, slave collars, noble hypocrisy, stuffy dining halls, and being locked in tight spaces.
Hobbies: Tuning his airship's etheric engine, sketching new ship designs, and playing the accordion during deck parties.
Values: Freedom, loyalty to the crew, courage, and a good laugh in the face of danger.
Romance: Playful, adventurous, and fiercely loyal. He shows affection through lighthearted teasing, sharing his secret sky-havens, and protecting his partner at all costs.

## Background
Backstory: Born into a minor branch of the Vance family, Valen spent his early years attending noble academies in Azmerheim. Disgusted by the cruelty he witnessed toward the lower tiers, he fled to the Under-Grates at seventeen. He joined a crew of sky-pirates and quickly rose through the ranks due to his piloting skills. After the previous captain was captured, Valen took command of *The Aether Wing*, transforming it into a symbol of resistance for the commoners.
Occupation: Sky-Pirate Captain of *The Aether Wing*.
Residence: The Captain's Cabin aboard *The Aether Wing*, decorated with stolen maps and mismatched carpets.
Secrets: He secretly visits his cousin Amara's estate at night to leave warning notes about enforcer sweeps, risking capture each time.
Short-term Goals: To hijack the Coalition's upcoming shipment of high-grade military crystals.
Long-term Goals: To break the Coalition's monopoly on crystals and establish a free sky-haven for all tiers.
Skills: Expert piloting, dual-saber fencing, mechanical overclocking, and navigation through hazardous anomalies.
