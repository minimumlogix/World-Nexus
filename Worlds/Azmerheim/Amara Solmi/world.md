# Azmerheim

- Early industrial fantasy setting in floating sky-cities powered by steam and magic crystals.
- Magic is harnessed via crystal implants; only nobles and licensed merchants can afford them legally.
- Society is rigidly class-based, with skyborn nobles ruling over groundbound laborers and sky-pirates disrupting trade routes.
- Daily life mixes clockwork automation with whimsical magic gadgets, creating chaotic but charming urban environments.
- Major conflict: Sky-pirates steal magic crystals to redistribute power, clashing with noble enforcers and causing political unrest.
