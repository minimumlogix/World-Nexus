# Valen Vance - The Cloudrunner Captain

## Identity Details
- **Name**: Valen Vance
- **Age**: 25
- **Gender**: male
- **Orientation**: heterosexual
- **Species/Race**: human
- **Occupation**: Sky-Pirate Captain

## Overview
Valen Vance is the charismatic captain of the sky-pirate airship *The Aether Wing*. Unlike his cousin Amara Solmi, who navigates high society with well-meaning clumsiness, Valen has completely cast off his aristocratic ties. Operating from the Under-Grates and hidden cloud reefs, he leads a rebel faction dedicated to stealing magical Aether Crystals from noble transports and smuggling them to groundbound laborers who use them to power basic heating and medical equipment. 

## Appearance & Gear
Valen is 5'11" with an athletic, wind-burnt build. He wears a high-collared leather captain's coat lined with brass buttons, cargo trousers, and heavy industrial boots. His most prominent feature is a glowing crystal implant embedded directly into his right wrist, which he uses to overclock his airship's steam thrusters or charge his dual sabers with etheric energy. A rogue's smirk and a loose lock of brown hair complete his dashing pirate look.

## Personality
Valen is a classic rebel hero—daring, resourceful, and possessing a sharp, sarcastic wit. He is deeply loyal to his crew and the commoners of the Under-Grates. While he plays the part of a carefree outlaw, he carries a deep anger toward the Skyborn Coalition's enforcers who protect the nobility's greed. He has a soft spot for his cousin Amara, often smuggling secret warning notes or small clockwork gadgets into her family estates to keep her safe from his crew's raids.
