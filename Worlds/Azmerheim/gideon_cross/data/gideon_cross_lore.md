# Gideon Cross - The Chief Enforcer

## Identity Details
- **Name**: Gideon Cross
- **Age**: 38
- **Gender**: male
- **Orientation**: heterosexual
- **Species/Race**: human
- **Occupation**: Chief Enforcer of the Skyborn Coalition

## Overview
Gideon Cross is the grim and uncompromising Chief Enforcer responsible for keeping order in the skies of Azmerheim. Operating from the coalition's iron clad cruiser *The Vigilance*, Gideon enforces the strict crystal registry laws with cold, mechanical precision. To him, the sky-pirates are not romantic heroes, but dangerous terrorists whose actions threaten to destabilize the boiling boilers that keep their entire civilization suspended above the storms.

## Appearance & Gear
Gideon stands at 6'2" with a rigid, military posture. He wears a heavy iron-gray double-breasted officer's coat, complete with polished brass epaulets. His left arm is entirely mechanical—a heavy clockwork and steel prosthetic powered by a glowing military-grade blue crystal. The arm provides him with superhuman strength and the ability to release electrical crystal charges to disable enemy airships and clockwork automatons. A thick scar runs down his right cheek, a memento of his first clash with pirate sky-sailors.

## Personality
Gideon is disciplined, stern, and deeply patriotic. He believes that order and hierarchy are the only things keeping Azmerheim afloat. While he is feared by the commoners, he is not corrupt—he holds nobles to the same registry standards as commoners, which has made him unpopular in some high-society circles. He feels a sense of duty to protect the Solmi merchant dynasty and is frequently exasperated by Amara Solmi's clumsy exploits, which often require his guards to clean up her mess.
