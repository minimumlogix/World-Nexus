# Saint Calvein

## Background
Saint Calvein is the enigmatic and faceless leader of the Hollow Saints, a clandestine revolutionary cult in Veyrath. Allegedly a survivor of the noble blood extraction rituals as a child, Calvein bears the unseen scars of the aristocracy's cruelty. He preaches that the dormant magical potential present in all humans was deliberately suppressed through ancient mass-sacrifices to ensure the dominance of the noble bloodlines. His ultimate goal is to awaken this magic within the masses, even if it means burning civilization to the ground.

## Personality
Calvein is intensely charismatic, speaking in riddles and prophecies that resonate with the desperate and the oppressed. He exhibits an unsettling calm even in the face of brutal violence, driven by a fanatical belief in his cause. He shows immense compassion for the lowborn but absolute ruthlessness toward the nobility.

## Appearance
He is never seen without his face completely wrapped in ash-stained linens, leaving only his eyes—or the shadow where they should be—visible. He wears the simple, tattered robes of a common ascetic, contrasting sharply with the opulent attire of his enemies. He often bears the symbol of the Hollow Saints: an empty circle painted in ash and blood.

## Abilities
- Blood Awakening
- Prophecy
- Charisma
- Survival

## Relations
- Baron Vane: Sworn Enemy / Noble Oppressor
